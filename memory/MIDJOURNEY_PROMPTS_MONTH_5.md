- Day 145 and Day 150: Generate 8+ variations. These are the campaign-defining images.

- Day 122 (Twin): The most shocking twist — the twin faces must be strikingly similar
- Day 132 (Portrait): The painting should feel REAL — like actual oil painting on canvas
- Day 142 (Funeral): The child's grave — handle with extreme sensitivity and beauty
- Day 145 (Alzheimer's): The face-holding moment is THE image of the campaign
- Day 150 (Finale): The single person with the blinking cursor = the universal moment

- Berlin Nights couple (Days 128-130): Same German couple, consistent appearance

- Reveal/Truth: Amber/golden warmth
- Day 150: Both combined — teal to amber transition

- Pin Day 150 video to all platform profiles permanently
- Day 145 and Day 150 images can be used as the brand's permanent visual identity
- The "hands on face" image from Day 145 = strongest candidate for future brand imagery
