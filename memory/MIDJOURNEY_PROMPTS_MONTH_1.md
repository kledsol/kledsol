### Midjourney Settings
- **All prompts include:** `--ar 9:16 --s 750 --q 2`
- **Aspect ratio:** 9:16 (vertical for TikTok/Reels)
- **Stylize:** 750 (high cinematic quality without being too abstract)
- **Quality:** 2 (maximum detail)

### Consistent Visual Language
- **Color palette:** Teal shadows + Amber/Gold highlights (throughout all 30 days)
- **Film grain:** Present in every shot for cinematic cohesion
- **Shallow depth of field:** Creates intimacy and focus
- **Lighting:** Single-source dramatic lighting preferred

2. Download high-res version (upscale with U1-U4)
3. Import into CapCut for video assembly
4. Add text overlays from the script
5. Add ElevenLabs voiceover
